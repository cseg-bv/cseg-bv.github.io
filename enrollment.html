Enrollment

Guidelines

Christian, faith-based educational program.

Appropriate attire & behavior is required.

For grades K-6 a parent is required to be onsite during classes.

Attendance is not required, but students will benefit by keeping up with the curriculum schedule.

Please speak with us about how we can best accommodate your family's unique needs.
