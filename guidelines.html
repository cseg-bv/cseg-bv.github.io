Guidelines & Policies

CSEG Policies


Christian, faith-based educational program.

Appropriate attire & behavior is required.

Parents are required to be onsite during classes.

Parents and infants (under 4 years of age) may attend classes, if space is available.

Each student must have their own curriculum & lab materials.

Pre-enrollment is required each semester.

Attendance is not required, but students are expected to keep up with the curriculum schedule.

Our program is synchronized with the CC F/E program held on the same day at the same facility, allowing parents access to the childcare & playground.
