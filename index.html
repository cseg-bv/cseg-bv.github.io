CSEG Overview

  • Offering in-person classes meeting one day per week (Mondays)
  • Grade K-6 Classes
    • Grades K-6 meet in the morning for General Education (Literature, Art & Science)
    • Grades 4-6 meet in the afternoon for Writing & Grammar
  • Grade 7-9 Classes
    • Grades 7-9 meet in the morning for Humanities & Social Sciences (HSS)
    • Grades 7-9 meet in the afternoon for Science, Technology, Engineering & Mathematics (STEM)
  • Class Offerings & Fees: Fall 2026-Spring 2027

CSEG Principles

  • Christ Led
    • Christian STEM Education Group is dedicated to glorifying God through a Christ-centered homeschooling community.
  • Parent Directed
    • As a parent-led community we believe that parents are the primary educators of their children.
    • Parents play an active role in our community by choosing curriculum and sharing their gifts through teaching and leadership.
  • Flexibility
    • Students can enroll in only the morning program, only the afternoon program, or both.
    • Attendance is not required, but students will benefit by keeping up with the curriculum schedule.
  • Low Cost
    • We are operating as a not-for-profit organization to serve our local homeschool community.
    • Program fees will only be collected to cover liability insurance, facility tithing & teacher salaries.
    • Curriculum & lab materials are purchased separately by the student.
