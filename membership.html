Membership

Membership is for students ages 12-18 years of age (7th-12th grade equivalent).

Enrollment occurs prior to the beginning of each semester.

Enrolled students can take an a la carte approach to attending classes in their program.

Attendance is not required, but students are expected to keep up with the curriculum schedule.
