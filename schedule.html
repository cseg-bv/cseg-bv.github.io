Curriculum

Morning Companion classes will have required curriculum (details TBD). A detailed calendar of each week's lessons will be provided, and students will participate in a mock trial at the end of the semester.


Afternoon S.T.E.M. classes will have required curriculum (details TBD) which will include lab kits on certain dates. A detailed calendar of each week's lessons & required lab kits will be provided.


The hybrid mathematics group activity accommodates students of all levels (arithmetic thru calculus), allowing students to grow at their own level while in a group setting with peers. In the hybrid mathematics tutoring session students independently work on their own curriculum with tutors available for help.
